Firefox Global Impact: 2002 2024

Explore Firefox's major milestones and privacy features over the years.

2002: Firefox Project Launched

Focus on creating an alternative to Internet Explorer and enhancing web standards.

2004: Firefox 1.0 Released

Introduced tabbed browsing, changing the way users navigated the web.

2005-2006: Extensions Introduced

Enabled users to customize their browsing experience with add-ons.

2009: HTML5 Support

Firefox supported HTML5 to push forward open web standards and media features.

2011: Do Not Track

Firefox introduced the first Do Not Track feature for enhanced privacy.

2015: Enhanced Tracking Protection

Introduced features that block third-party cookies and improve privacy.

2017: Firefox Quantum Released

Boosted speed and efficiency with a new rendering engine.

2018: Tracking Protection Enhanced

By default, blocks third-party cookies, further enhancing user privacy.

2020: Firefox Monitor

Launched Firefox Monitor to help users check if their data was involved in a breach.

2021: Total Cookie Protection

Prevents cross-site tracking through isolated cookies for better privacy.

2024: Continued Advocacy for Open Web & Privacy

Firefox continues to champion an open, user-friendly, and private web experience.
